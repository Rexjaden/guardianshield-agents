## üÜò Troubleshooting & Support

### Common Issues

**Low Engagement:**
- Increase posting frequency
- Try different content types
- Engage with relevant conversations
- Run polls and challenges
- Collaborate with influencers

**Negative Feedback:**
- Respond quickly and professionally
- Acknowledge concerns
- Share what you're doing to address
- Take conversations private when appropriate
- Learn and improve

**Technical Issues:**
- Have backup communication channels
- Update status page immediately
- Provide regular updates
- Be transparent about timeline
- Follow up after resolution

**Growth Plateau:**
- Analyze what changed
- Try new content formats
- Launch special campaigns
- Increase outreach efforts
- Double down on what works
