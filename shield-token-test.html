Shield Token (SI) - ERC-8055

The Shield Token (SI) represents a revolutionary breakthrough in blockchain security through our custom ERC-8055 standard. Each token contains a unique serial number for precise tracking and features an advanced anti-fraud mechanism: if acquired through fraudulent means, the token is automatically burned and reminted with its original serial number, then placed in our treasury for the rightful owner to reclaim. This ensures complete protection and recovery of assets.
