**GuardianShield Data Lifecycle Management**  
**Effective Date:** January 14, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 14, 2026

## Policy Overview

This Data Retention Policy defines how GuardianShield collects, stores, manages, and disposes of data throughout its lifecycle. Our policy balances operational needs, legal requirements, and user privacy rights.

**Scope:** This policy applies to all data collected, processed, and stored by GuardianShield systems and services.

---

## Data Categories and Retention Periods

### User Account Data

**Personal Information**
- **Retention Period:** Active account + 7 years after closure
- **Data Types:** Name, email, phone, address, identification documents
- **Legal Basis:** Contract performance, legal obligations, tax requirements
- **Disposal Method:** Secure deletion with cryptographic verification

**Authentication Data**
- **Retention Period:** Active account + 90 days after closure
- **Data Types:** Password hashes, MFA tokens, security questions
- **Legal Basis:** Contract performance, security
- **Disposal Method:** Immediate secure overwriting

### Financial and Payment Data

**Transaction Records**
- **Retention Period:** 7 years from transaction date
- **Data Types:** Payment history, invoices, receipts, tax records
- **Legal Basis:** Legal obligations, tax compliance
- **Disposal Method:** Secure archival then cryptographic deletion

### Platform Usage Data

**API Usage Logs**
- **Retention Period:** 90 days for detailed logs, 3 years for aggregated data
- **Data Types:** API calls, response times, error logs, usage statistics
- **Legal Basis:** Contract performance, service improvement
- **Disposal Method:** Standard deletion, anonymization for analytics

**Security Logs**
- **Retention Period:** 3 years minimum, 7 years for security incidents
- **Data Types:** Access logs, authentication attempts, security events
- **Legal Basis:** Security, legal obligations
- **Disposal Method:** Secure archival then deletion

### Threat Intelligence Data

**Threat Indicators**
- **Retention Period:** Indefinitely (anonymized after 3 years)
- **Data Types:** IP addresses, domains, file hashes, behavioral patterns
- **Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest, public safety
- **Disposal Method:** Anonymization, not deletion (valuable for future threat detection)

## User Rights and Requests

### Data Subject Requests

**Right to Access**
- **Data Export:** Complete data export in machine-readable format
- **Processing Timeline:** 30 days from verified request
- **Verification:** Identity verification before data release

**Right to Deletion**
- **Processing Timeline:** 30 days from verified request
- **Exceptions:** Legal obligations, legitimate interests
- **Confirmation:** Written confirmation of deletion completion

### California Residents (CCPA)

**Enhanced Rights**
- **Right to Know:** Detailed information about data collection and use
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

---

## Data Disposal Methods

### Technical Disposal
- **Cryptographic Erasure:** Deletion of encryption keys rendering data unreadable
- **Multi-Pass Overwriting:** DoD 5220.22-M standard overwriting
- **Anonymization:** Removal of personally identifiable information

### Legal Compliance
- **Legal Hold:** Suspension of deletion for legal proceedings
- **Tax Records:** 7-year retention for tax purposes
- **Regulatory Requirements:** SEC and banking regulation compliance
