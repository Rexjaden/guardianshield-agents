## CCPA Rights for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This notice describes your CCPA rights and how to exercise them.

### Your Rights Include:

#### 1. Right to Know
- **Categories of Personal Information:** What personal information we collect
- **Sources:** Where we collect personal information from
- **Business Purposes:** Why we collect and use personal information
- **Third Parties:** Who we share personal information with
- **Specific Information:** Request specific pieces of personal information we have about you

#### 2. Right to Delete
- **Deletion Request:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Verification:** We verify your identity before processing deletion requests
- **Exceptions:** Some information may be retained for legal or business purposes
- **Confirmation:** We confirm completion of deletion requests

#### 3. Right to Opt-Out
- **Sale of Information:** We do NOT sell personal information
- **Sharing:** We may share information with service providers
- **Third-Party Analytics:** You can opt-out of certain analytics cookies
- **Marketing:** Opt-out of marketing communications

#### 4. Right to Non-Discrimination
- **Equal Service:** We will not discriminate for exercising CCPA rights
- **No Penalties:** No denial of services, different pricing, or reduced quality
- **Incentive Programs:** May offer incentives for data collection (optional)
